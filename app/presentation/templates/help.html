Core Management

Purpose: System administration and foundation data

  • Users: Manage system users and permissions
  • Locations: Define where assets are stored
  • Asset Types: Categorize different types of assets
  • Make/Models: Define specific asset models
  • Assets: Core asset records and basic information
  • Events: Track all system activities
Asset Management

Purpose: Detailed asset lifecycle and tracking

  • Asset Overview: Dashboard and statistics
  • Asset Details: Purchase info, registration, warranty
  • Model Details: Technical specifications
  • Asset Dashboard: Comprehensive asset analytics
  • Quick Actions: Common asset operations

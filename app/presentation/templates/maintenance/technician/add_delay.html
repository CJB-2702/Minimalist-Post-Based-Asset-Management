About Delays

Report delays when maintenance work cannot proceed as scheduled. This helps management track issues and make informed decisions.

Common Delay Types
  • Parts: Waiting for parts to arrive
  • Weather: Weather conditions prevent work
  • Equipment: Required equipment unavailable
  • Access: Cannot access work area
  • Safety: Safety concerns preventing work
Resolving Delays

Once the delay is resolved, you can mark it as complete from the delays page. This updates the maintenance status automatically.
